Our Programs

Discipleship Training

Weekly classes that dive deep into the Word of God and Christian doctrine, helping believers grow strong spiritual roots and lead with wisdom.

Youth Mentorship

A powerful initiative connecting teens and young adults with trusted mentors who guide them in faith, life, education, and personal development.

Women's Empowerment Circle

A safe, empowering space for women to gather, support each other, study Scripture, and rise together in faith and leadership.

Outreach & Evangelism

Hands-on community engagement: street ministry, food drives, prison visits, and neighborhood outreach to share Christ’s love in action.

Creative Arts Ministry

Drama, dance, spoken word, music, and media — a vibrant program where creativity is used to glorify God and express faith artistically.
