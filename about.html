Who We Are

Our Story

VWO-karati Church was founded on the belief that faith should be lived boldly, inclusively, and joyfully. From our humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a vibrant community where people of all walks of life can encounter God, grow in discipleship, and make a lasting impact in the world.

We are rooted in Scripture, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and committed to walking in the way of Jesus. Every service, small group, and outreach is designed to point people toward grace, transformation, and belonging.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To lead people into a transformative relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, teaching, community, and service.

Vision: A world where every soul knows their identity in Christ, and every community is transformed through love, truth, and justice.

Core Values That Guide Us

Faith

We are anchored in unwavering belief in the Gospel of Christ, trusting God in all seasons and circumstances.

Community

We foster authentic relationships where people are seen, heard, and supported as part of God’s family.

Service

We live out our faith through generous acts of service, compassion, and social responsibility.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do—reflecting the glory of God through our work, worship, and witness.

Worship

Worship is the heartbeat of our church—intimate, expressive, and Spirit-led—drawing us closer to the presence of God.

Discipleship

We invest in spiritual growth through teaching, mentorship, and intentional life-on-life development.
